Dr. Manoj Kumar

Ph.D. IIT Guwahati

Dr. Manoj kumar is an Assistant professor and head of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Government Engineering College Arwal. He received his Ph.D. in 2023 from IIT Guwahati under the supervision of Dr. Pinaki Mitra and Dr. Aryabartta Sahu. He has completed his M-Tech in 2014 in Computer Science with specialization in Multimedia and Software Systems from NITTTR Kolkata. His current research area of interest include GPU Computing , Meta-heuristics on GPU, High Performance Computing, and Soft computing.

Research Interest: GPU Computing , Meta-heuristics on GPU, High Performance Computing, Soft Computing.
